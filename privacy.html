Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 9, 2025 Last Updated: May 9, 2025

1. Overview

GR8 Web Design ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide when you visit our website or inquire about our services. By using our website, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when submitting a quote request or contacting us. This may include:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Business name
  • Project details and budget range
  • Any additional information you include in your message

We do not collect payment information through our website. All billing is handled through secure third-party payment processors.

We may also automatically collect non-personal technical data when you visit our site, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (mobile, tablet, desktop)
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website or source
  • General geographic location (city/region level only)

3. How We Use Your Information

Information you provide is used exclusively to:

  • Respond to your quote request or inquiry
  • Communicate with you about your project
  • Send project updates, invoices, and service-related communications
  • Improve our website and service offerings

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes — ever.

4. How We Store and Protect Your Information

Your information is transmitted via our contact form and delivered securely to our Google Workspace business email account using Gmail SMTP. We take reasonable precautions to protect your data from unauthorized access, including:

  • SSL/HTTPS encryption on all site traffic
  • Secure, password-protected business email accounts
  • Limited access — only Hardel Santos-Sanchez, the sole owner of GR8 Web Design, has access to your submitted information

6. Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to analyze site traffic and usage patterns. These cookies do not collect personally identifiable information.

You may disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Doing so will not affect your ability to use our website.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information from our records
  • Opt out of any future communications from us at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
