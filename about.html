Meet the Founder

Hardel Santos

Founder · Designer · Developer · Your Single Point of Contact

Hi, I'm Hardel Santos — the founder, designer, developer, and single point of contact behind GR8 Web Design. I'm a Northern Virginia local, a husband, and a proud father to my son. Outside of building websites, family is everything to me — and that same sense of care and responsibility carries directly into how I run this business.

I studied Business Administration with a concentration in Management Information Systems — a background that bridges both strategic thinking and technical execution. I'm also an AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner, which means I understand cloud architecture and can ensure your website is deployed on infrastructure that's reliable, scalable, and built to last.

My entire professional career has been spent as a federal contractor serving two of the most mission-critical agencies in the country — the Department of Veterans Affairs and the General Services Administration. In that time, I focused on improving systems, enhancing user experience, strengthening security, and sharpening communication between teams and the people they serve.

That background isn't just a resume line. It means when I build your website, I'm thinking about how real people use it, how secure it is, how well it communicates your message, and how smoothly every piece of it works together. That's the standard I was trained to hold — and it's the standard I bring to every client.
